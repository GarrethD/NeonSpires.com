About Neonspires

NeonSpire Studios is an indie game studio focused on creating immersive, nostalgic experiences inspired by classic games. Our mission is to bring back the charm of the retro era while delivering stories that resonate with modern audiences. Cloaked Protocol is our passion project, designed as a tribute to the stealth-action classics we grew up loving.
