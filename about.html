About NeonSpires Studios

Welcome to NeonSpires Studios, an indie game development studio dedicated to crafting immersive and nostalgic gaming experiences. We specialize in bringing the retro gaming charm of the PS1 era into the modern age with a fresh twist. Our flagship title, Cloaked Protocol, is a stealth action espionage game that captures the spirit of classic games, featuring pixel art, a retro-inspired soundtrack, and engaging gameplay.

At NeonSpires, we are passionate about delivering rich narratives that resonate with today’s audience. Inspired by the golden age of gaming, our mission is to merge retro aesthetics with modern gameplay elements to create truly unforgettable experiences. Explore more about Cloaked Protocol here.

Our Passion Project: Cloaked Protocol

Cloaked Protocol is our love letter to the stealth-action classics of the 90s, combining deep storytelling with tactical espionage. Set in a dystopian future, players will navigate a world of deception, uncover hidden conspiracies, and outsmart enemies using strategy and stealth. The game features stunning PS1-style graphics, stealth mechanics, and challenging missions, designed to engage players in a world where appearances are deceiving.
