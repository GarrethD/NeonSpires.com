About NeonSpires Studio

NeonSpires Studio is a one-person indie studio dedicated to making the kind of games that stick with you long after you put down the controller.
NeonSpires is all about exploring new ideas, surprising genres, and capturing the thrill and emotion of gaming’s best moments—whether it’s a retro throwback, a modern experiment, or something in between.
Every project is built from the ground up by one developer, drawing from a broad mix of inspirations and styles. The goal? Games that feel personal, handcrafted, and just a little bit different from the usual.
While NeonSpires doesn’t stick to a single art style or genre, you’ll always find a commitment to atmosphere, story, and gameplay. Sometimes nostalgic, sometimes weird, sometimes bold—always indie.
Thanks for stopping by. Whether you’re here for a game, a vibe, or just to support solo creators—welcome!
