In a dystopian future, the United Nations projects an image of global peace and unity, promising a brighter tomorrow for all. Nations appear to be working together, and prosperity seems to be within everyone's reach. But beneath this polished surface lies a different reality—one where control is absolute, and freedom is a carefully curated illusion. The UN and its powerful allies manipulate events from the shadows, fueling hidden conflicts and suppressing any threats to their authority.

Cloaked Protocol by NeonSpires Studios is a stealth action espionage game that reimagines the classic PS1 aesthetic for modern audiences. With pixel art graphics, a retro soundtrack, and challenging gameplay, it offers players a nostalgic yet refreshing experience. Navigate intricate levels, outsmart enemies, and uncover a deep conspiracy in a world where deception is at its core. Learn more about NeonSpires here.
