In a dystopian future, the United Nations projects an image of global peace and unity, promising a brighter tomorrow for all. Nations appear to be working together, and prosperity seems to be within everyone's reach. But beneath this polished surface lies a different reality—one where control is absolute, and freedom is a carefully curated illusion. The UN and its powerful allies manipulate events from the shadows, fueling hidden conflicts and suppressing any threats to their authority. While the public remains oblivious to the true nature of their rulers, those who dig deeper discover a world on the brink, where deception and power struggles shape the fate of humanity

NeonSpires' Cloaked Protocol is a stealth action espionage game that reimagines the classic PS1 aesthetic for a modern audience. With its pixel art graphics, retro soundtrack, and challenging gameplay, the game offers a nostalgic yet fresh experience. Players will navigate intricate levels, outsmart enemies, and uncover a deep conspiracy in a world where appearances can be deceiving.
