About NeonSpires

NeonSprire Studios (NeonSpires in short) is a solo-managed indie game studio developing Cloaked Protocol. This passion project is a homage to Metal Gear Solid 1. NeonSpires will in the future open the doors to help if willing.

Cloaked Protocol - Story

The United Nations has resolved a peaceful solution to human indulgence and prolonging the lifespan of all nations. The Tribunal Nation States have imposed absolute will on all people through education, communication, and medical care. The people are vastly unaware of the control that the Tribunal Nation States have, and with the enforcement of the United Nations, they continue to pull strings, harbor wars, and create full-scale global collapse just to maintain control.

At any time, there are dozens of wars carefully controlled by the will of the council of thirteen. Thirteen members of the United Nations and Tribunal Nation States, where they operate with impunity and have total immunity to all actions the general population would determine to be inhumane and war crimes.
